Biography

I am an Associate Professor at Shandong Normal University. I received the Ph. D. degree in communication and information systems from Shandong University under supervision of Prof. Haixia Zhang. Now I am working at the Communication Theory Lab (CTL) of King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) with Prof. Mohamed-Slim Alouini as a visiting scholar. My research mainly focus on physical layer security and relay communications. Specially, I am very interested in the security problem in Terahertz band.

What's New

  • Aug. 30, 2019: I am now in KAUST as a visiting scholar working with Prof. Mohamed-Slim Alouini.
